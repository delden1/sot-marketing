Features

Everything you need to track a real portfolio.

Built around what individual investors actually do — long stock positions, multi-leg options, dividend income, and corporate events. No fluff.

Real-time stocks & options

Track open and closed positions across stocks and multi-leg option trades. Live quotes refresh through the day; option chains update with mid + IV + Greeks.

Allocation breakdown

Click into sector, industry, market cap, country, currency, exchange, security type, or strategy. Filters cross-link, so picking "Financials" reshapes every other dimension.

Performance vs. benchmarks

See your total return against the S&P 500, Nasdaq, and MSCI All-World — daily, weekly, monthly. Cumulative line, period bars, or both at once.

Dividend tracking + PADI

Per-period dividend income, projected annual dividend income (PADI), 1Y / 3Y / 5Y growth rates per holding, and a forward-looking projection chart.

Corporate event calendar

Earnings, ex-dividend dates, dividend pay dates, splits, and macro events in a clean Beleggen-style calendar — plus a per-ticker timeline in the company drawer.

Worth + deposits view

See your portfolio value over time alongside cumulative cash deposits — so you can tell what's market gain vs. what you've actually put in. Hover any date for the full breakdown.

Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Each user's positions are isolated at the database level — no other user can read your data, and we don't sell or share it. The tracker is built specifically for personal use, not as a social trading platform.
How do I get my positions in?
Three ways: type them in directly (fastest for a small portfolio), paste a CSV from your broker, or upload a screenshot of your broker dashboard — the importer reads it with AI vision and stages every row for review before it lands in your portfolio.
Which exchanges and brokers are supported?
Any ticker that EODHD covers — that includes US (NYSE, Nasdaq), Canada (TSX, TSX-V), UK (LSE), most major European exchanges (Euronext, Xetra, Borsa Italiana), Australia (ASX), and dozens more. Multi-currency holdings are converted to USD with daily FX rates.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Both plans start with a 7-day free trial — cancel during the trial and you're charged nothing. After the trial, subscriptions are managed through Stripe and you can cancel from your account page in one click. Monthly subscribers stop being charged at the end of their billing cycle; annual subscribers keep access through their paid year.
How is this different from my broker's dashboard?
Brokers show what's in their account. If you split capital across multiple brokers (or have a self-managed pension and a personal account), you don't get a unified view. SoT Portfolio Tracker is broker-agnostic: type in everything once, see it all together, with proper performance attribution and corporate-event tracking that most brokers don't offer.
Does it work on mobile?
It's optimized for desktop right now (where you actually do portfolio analysis). It's usable on tablet/phone, but the data tables are best read on a wide screen.

See it before you commit.

The live demo loads a real, fully-populated portfolio so you can click around the doughnuts, hover the charts, and read the tables. No signup, no card.
